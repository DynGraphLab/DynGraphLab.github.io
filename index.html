DynGraphLab

Dynamic Graph Repository by M. Henzinger, K. Hanauer and C. Schulz

Welcome to the dynamic graph repository. The goal here is to explicitly provide fully-dynamic real-world and synthetic instance, i.e. instances that have both, insertion and deletion operations. We are still looking for more fully dynamic instances, in particular those based on real-world applications. If you can share your data, please contact us or issue a pull request here -- we will then provide your instance on our servers. We will also list more instances over time. Your contribution is very much appreciated!
