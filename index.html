DynGraphLab

Dynamic Graph Repository by M. Henzinger, K. Hanauer and C. Schulz

Welcome to the dynamic graph repository. The goal here is to explicitly provide fully-dynamic real-world, i.e. instances that have insertion and deletion operations. In some cases, the network has been insertion only and has been augmented by random deletions. We are still looking for more fully dynamic instances, in particular those based on real-world applications. If you can share your data, please contact us or issue a pull request here -- we will then provide your instance on our servers. We will also list more instances over time. Your contribution is very much appreciated!

How to Cite the Downloads

If you use these data in your research, please cite the following paper as well as the original source:
@inproceedings{HanHenSchuSurvey2021,
